About the Webinar

Is Form 1 a key to a locked door? If so, why does this door remain closed for so many individuals suffering from serious mental health challenges? The uncomfortable truth is that mental disorders are often significantly overlooked or ignored in assessments for attendant care needs. Moreover, the detection and analysis are in the hands of healthcare providers who, despite their expertise, may lack the specific qualifications required to evaluate the complexities of mental health conditions. Join us and be part of an uncomfortable yet impactful conversation! This conversation will help all individuals with serious mental health disorders receive the identification and care they rightfully deserve.
